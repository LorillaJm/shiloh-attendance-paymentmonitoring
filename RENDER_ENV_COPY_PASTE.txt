========================================
HOW TO ADD IN RENDER:
========================================
1. Go to Render Dashboard
2. Click your web service
3. Click "Environment" tab
4. Click "Add Environment Variable"
5. Copy each line above (format: KEY=VALUE)
6. Paste Key in "Key" field
7. Paste Value in "Value" field
8. Click "Save Changes"
9. Render will auto-redeploy

========================================
VERIFY AFTER DEPLOY:
========================================
1. Visit: https://shiloh-attendance-paymentmonitoring.onrender.com/check-env.php
2. Check all URLs show "https://"
3. Test login at /admin/login
4. Check browser console for errors
5. Check Render logs if issues persist
